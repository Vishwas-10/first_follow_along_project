Spooky is the primary recipe developer, content creator, and food photographer behind yummy.eats. He has been blogging in some form since 2010 and developed a deep love for recipe experimentation and food photography in the process. Spooky’s food philosophy has evolved greatly over the last several years, and He now enjoys a diverse diet free from labels. He is a food stylist, photographer, and the author of yummy.eats’s Everyday Cooking. He’s also an aspiring yogi, lover of all things green, and his go-to morning drink is a creamy matcha latte.
